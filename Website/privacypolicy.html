AMIA Studios – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 06, 2025

At AMIA Studios, we’re committed to protecting your privacy. Here’s how we handle your information when you visit our site or create an account:

  • What We Collect:

  • Basic Data: We may collect your IP address, device info, or usage patterns when you browse or use our mini apps.
  • Account Info: If you sign up for an account, we’ll collect details like your email address, username, and a securely hashed password. You can optionally provide extra info (e.g., a profile name), but it’s not required.

  • How We Use It:

  • We use this data to run the site, manage your account, improve our tools, and fix issues. For example, your email might be used to send login confirmations or account updates.
  • We don’t sell or share your personal info with anyone, period.

  • Cookies: We use cookies to keep you logged in, remember your settings, or enhance your experience. You can disable them in your browser, but some features (like staying logged in) might not work as well.

  • Third Parties: If our site links to external services (e.g., for app features), their privacy policies apply when you use them. We’re not responsible for their practices.

  • Account Security: You’re responsible for keeping your login details safe. We’ll do our part with secure storage and encryption, but let us know right away if something seems off.

  • Data Deletion: Want your account gone? Contact us, and we’ll delete your info (unless we need to keep some for legal reasons).

  • Changes: We’ll update this policy if things change. Check back here to stay informed.

    • Questions? Hit us up via our Contact page.
